# Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** February 05, 2026 Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains, in plain language, how we handle your phone number and SMS messages. --- ## What We Collect We only collect your phone number when **you** give it to us — for example, when you: - Make a transaction - Send us an inquiry - Sign up to receive text notifications ## How We Use Your Number We use your phone number to send you SMS messages you've opted into. Your number is only shared with our SMS delivery provider so they can send messages on our behalf (subject to their own privacy policy). **Note:** Standard messaging and data rates from your carrier may apply. ## How to Opt Out Want to stop receiving messages? Simply **text STOP** at any time. Once you opt out: - We will stop sending you SMS messages. - We will **not** sell or transfer your phone number to anyone. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll post a notice so you're always informed. --- **Questions?** Contact us at c-assistsupport@protonmail.com. We're happy to help.
